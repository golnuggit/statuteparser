26 U.S.C. ยง 162 Business expenses.
(a) In general. Business expenses deductible from gross income include the ordinary and necessary expenses paid or incurred during the taxable year in carrying on any trade or business.
(b) Exceptions. For purposes of paragraph (a), the following apply:
(1) Subject to section 274, no deduction shall be allowed for certain entertainment expenses.
(2) Except as provided in paragraph (a)(1), additional amounts may be allowed when otherwise authorized.
